We are having a daytime wedding on Saturday, 9/24, with an optional (but highly recommended) after-party that same night.

The Wedding

The ceremony and reception will start at 11:30 AM and end in the late afternoon (around 4:30 PM). The wedding venue is open to public so, guests can stay on the venue for a few more hours and soak in the Hudson river views if they wish to do so.

After-Partaay

We will be holding a chill backyard party starting 7:30 PM in Rhinebeck, NY with homemade beer, pizza, s'mores, bonfires, whiskey shots and dancing.

Accomodation

We did not reserve hotel blocks, because all of them had 2-night requirements and were prohibitively expensive. We recommend using airbnb, homeaway, or vrbo to book inexpensive accomodation in any of the following locations: Tivoli, Rhinebeck, Annandale-on-Hudson, Clermont, Barrytown, Red Hook, Livingston, and Hudson

AirBnb has a beautiful rustic barn, a refurbished chicken coop, and a yurt, among more traditional options.

Feel free to check in with us for advice on where to stay.

Travel to Clermont

The Clermont Historic Site is a 2 hour drive from Manhattan via the I-87 N. There is ample parking available at the wedding venue for all who are driving in. We will update this space with further details on local taxi services in case anyone needs them.

Travel to the After Partaaay

The after party will be at 111 Burger Rd. in Rhinebeck, NY. The house is between a 30 to 5 minute drive from all locations suggested for accomodation. And has ample parking.

Things to do in NY

Hudson Valley has something for everyone -- hiking, antiquing, sculpture gardens, spas, and plenty of food. Here are a few places to visit on your way up or down:

Storm King Art Center: Iconic outdoor sculpture museum in New Windsor, NY. You could spend hours here. It's far from the venue, but a good stop on the way up.

Atlantic Kayak Tours: Go for a ride on the Hudson River!

Drink wine! The Hudson Valley region is home to a number of wineries.

Hike! Use the link to access information on all of the great hikes in the Hudson Valley.

Wander through adorable towns. Hudson, 20 minutes north of the wedding venue, has great food, fun stores, and a cool bar/music venue called Helsinki Hudson. You can also check out the food struck scene. If you're up for a little more of a drive, you're only about 30 minutes away from Woodstock. Relive your youth, or, if you couldn't possibly have been there, put on your best flower crown and brush up on Jimi.

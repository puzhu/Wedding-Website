Accomodation near Germantown

Our wedding is in the height of the fall season in a beautiful part of Hudson Valley, so accommodations fill up quickly. We are having a daytime wedding on Saturday, 9/24, with an optional (but recommended) bonfire Saturday night. We did not reserve hotel blocks, because all of them had 2-night requirements and were prohibitively expensive. We recommend using airbnb, homeaway, or vrbo to book inexpensive accomodation in any of the following locations: Tivoli, Rhinebeck, Annandale-on-Hudson, Clermont, Barry, Red Hook, Livingston, and Hudson

AirBnb has a beautiful rustic barn, a refurbished chicken coop, and a yurt, among more traditional options.

Feel free to check in with us for advice on where to stay.

Travel to Clermont

The Clermont Historic Site is a 2 hour drive from Manhattan via the I-87 N. There is ample parking available at the wedding venue for all who are driving in. We will update this space with further details on local taxi services in case anyone needs them.
